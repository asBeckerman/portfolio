Christmas is a holiday common throughout Europe, where family comes together to celebrate

Christmas is a celebration of the birth of Jesus. This holiday is celecrated on the 25th of December. Family and friends come together to eat and exchange gifts.

Like the Chinese, the Taiwanese celebrate the Lunar New Year

The Lunar New Year is a celebration of the New Year, based on the lunar calendar. Parades and spectacles appear in the streets, and a fireworks show is put on. Kids are given red envelopes containing money.
