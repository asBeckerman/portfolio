Clubs

Chinese Club

Meets every other Tuesday from 3:30-4:30 p.m. in Room 2812.

The Chinese Club is organized to allow students to spend time with other students who have an interest in the Chinese language and culture. Activities include attending Chinese films, visiting Chinatown, playing Chinese board games, celebrating Chinese festivals, and after-school cooking fun.

Sponsors: Ms. Ye li.ye1@d125.org
Ms. Wang jwang@d125.org

German Club

Meets Fridays at 7:50 a.m. in Room 2926

German club is an environment created for students who have an interest in learning about the German culture and language. Members of the club are responsible for trips, events, and activities for the German program. Fun activities we are involved in are World's Fair, movie night, interacting with German exchange students, German-American day, and much more.

Sponsors: Ms. Glowacka pglowacka@d125.org
